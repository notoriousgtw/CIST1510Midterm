Our Story

2018 — A hostel kitchen in Lisbon

Four strangers traded trail tips over midnight noodles. By sunrise, we were planning a shared route through Europe.

2019 — From night buses to new best friends

We learned that great trips don’t need complicated spreadsheets—just trusted recommendations and a flexible plan.

2022 — A proposal on a sleeper train

Somewhere between Vienna and Zurich, love officially joined the itinerary.

2025 — Plane & Simple takes off

We launched a boutique agency to share our simple formula: curated stays, transparent pricing, and real humans who care.
